Who We Are

A Family dedicated to hand raising Goldendoodles

Raised by all family members

Our puppies are raised by their parents as well as each member of our family. They spend time playing with our children and spend time with the grown up dogs to learn important social skills.

We love our dogs

We decided to help families find their new Goldendoodle puppies because we love the breed. We are dedicated to help them develope into the smart, loyal, kind, and gentle dogs we know they are.

Dedication to the breed

We have had several different breeds in our household and have loved all of our pets. However, there really is something special about a Goldendoodle that has really captured our hearts.
